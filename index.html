fire victims of maharashtra hospital

what has happened?

Fifteen COVID-19 patients were killed in a fire that swept through the intensive care unit of Vijay Vallabh Hospital in Virar, 50 km from Mumbai, early on Friday.17 patients were in the ICU on the second floor of the four-storey hospital when the fire broke out around 3 a.m. Three fire tenders reached the spot immediately, and the fire was doused by 5.20 a.m.While 15 patients died in this tragedy, the remaining patients were moved to other hospitals
